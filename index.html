Our Story

At Temporal Treasures, we believe that measuring time should be as beautiful as time itself. Our artisans combine traditional craftsmanship with innovative designs to create sand clocks that are both functional timepieces and statement art pieces.

Each sand clock is meticulously handcrafted using carefully selected materials, from exotic hardwoods to hand-blown glass, filled with precisely calibrated colored sands that flow with mesmerizing grace.
